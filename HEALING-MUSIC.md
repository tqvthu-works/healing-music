# HEALING_MUSIC_RULES.md

## PURPOSE

This document defines the major healing music categories commonly used on YouTube, Spotify, meditation apps, sleep apps, wellness content, and music generation platforms.

The goal is NOT to imitate existing songs.

The goal is to generate music matching listener expectations for each healing category.

---

## GLOBAL NEGATIVE PROMPTS

Use these negative prompts across all categories to prevent unwanted audio artifacts or structural errors (unless specifically required by the genre, like beats in Lofi):

* drums, heavy percussion, aggressive beats
* vocals, human voice, choir singing words
* complex solos, electric guitar, heavy bass
* sudden volume spikes, tempo changes, sudden drops
* EDM elements, cinematic battle energy

# CATEGORY 01

# AMBIENT HEALING MUSIC

## DESCRIPTION

The most common modern healing music style.
Focuses on emotional calmness, spacious atmosphere, relaxation and mental recovery.
Usually contains minimal melody and slow movement.
Listeners use it for relaxation, stress relief, reading, mindfulness, and background listening.

## CORE CHARACTERISTICS

Atmosphere first. Melody second.
No dramatic changes. No tension building.
No aggressive rhythm. No strong percussion.

## COMMON INSTRUMENTS

* soft ambient pads
* warm synth textures
* gentle piano
* soft strings
* airy vocal textures
* subtle bells

## TEMPO

40-70 BPM or free-time ambient

## MOOD TAGS

peaceful, warm, gentle, calming, dreamlike, spacious, emotional, comforting

## SUNO KEYWORDS

ambient healing music, soft atmospheric pads, warm cinematic textures, gentle piano, peaceful soundscape, deep relaxation, mindfulness atmosphere

## TEMPLATE

Soft ambient healing music, warm atmospheric pads, gentle piano motifs, spacious reverb, peaceful emotional atmosphere, slow evolving textures, deep relaxation, mindfulness, calming cinematic ambience, no percussion.

# CATEGORY 02

# NATURE SOUNDSCAPE

## DESCRIPTION

Pure environmental audio.
Often contains no music at all.
Designed to recreate natural environments.

## COMMON ELEMENTS

* birds, forest ambience, river, waterfall
* rain, ocean waves, insects, wind

## PURPOSE

## AVOID

* musical instruments
* melodies
* rhythm
* synths
* vocals

## SUNO KEYWORDS

## TEMPLATE

# CATEGORY 03

# AMBIENT + NATURE HYBRID

## DESCRIPTION

Currently one of the most popular healing formats.
Combines ambient music with natural sounds. Performs better than pure ambient because listeners perceive stronger emotional immersion.

## STRUCTURE

Layer 1: ambient pads
Layer 2: soft piano
Layer 3: nature sounds
Layer 4: large atmospheric reverb

## COMMON NATURE ELEMENTS

* rain, river, waterfall, forest birds, wind, night insects

## SUNO KEYWORDS

hybrid ambient nature, soft piano and rain, forest soundscape with warm pads, seamless background loop, immersive atmospheric relaxation

## TEMPLATE

Soft ambient healing music, warm atmospheric pads, gentle piano, distant birds, flowing stream, subtle wind ambience, spacious reverb, emotional relaxation, meditation atmosphere, immersive nature soundscape, seamless loop.
